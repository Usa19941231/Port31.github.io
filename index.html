COVID 19
DATA EXPLORATION

This project analyzes COVID-19 data by comparing infection, death, and vaccination rates across countries and continents. It examines the likelihood of dying from COVID-19, the percentage of populations infected, and highlights regions with the highest infection and death rates. Additionally, advanced calculations using CTEs and Temporary Tables are employed, and vaccination data is analyzed to show what portion of populations received at least one vaccine dose.
